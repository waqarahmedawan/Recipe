Anda Paratha recipe

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup Wheat Flour
  • Water as required to knead the dough
  • Salt to taste in a dough
  • 2 Eggs
  • ½ Chopped Onions
  • ½ Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Coriander Leaves
  • 1 Chopped Green Chili
  • 1 inch Chopped Green Capsicum
  • 2 pinches Red Chili Powder
  • 2 pinches Salt
  • pinch Turmeric Powder

Instructions:

  1. First, to knead a dough take half a cup of wheat flour use water as required and salt to taste, and knead until it turns soft and smooth cover it, and set aside.
  2. Divide dough into equal parts and make a small smooth ball.
  3. Heat a pan place the paratha and half cook on a low flame and set aside.
  4. For egg mixture take two eggs, half chopped onion, half chopped tomato, two tablespoons chopped coriander leaves, one chopped green chili, one inch chopped green capsicum, 2 pinches red chili powder, two pinches salt, one pinch of turmeric powder whisk it nicely.
  5. Heat the pan add one teaspoon olive oil place the half cook paratha in it pour the egg mixture on the paratha cover the lid and let it cook for one minute then after one minute flips the paratha and cook the other side also for one minute in the same way on a low to medium flame until to ensure the egg is cook completely and paratha is perfectly crisp.
  6. Your egg (anda) paratha is ready you can enjoy this with any varieties of chutney, sauces, and tea.
